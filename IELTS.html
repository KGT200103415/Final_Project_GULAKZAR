IELTS exam preparation

If you are planning to study abroad or move to another country to work or live, you may be asked to prove your English language ability with the IELTS test.

The course from TELA give you all the information and practice you need to approach all four parts of IELTS with confidence that you can achieve the band score you need.

As well as that, each course looks at common problems language learners have with that language skill:

In this course you will:

Speaking:

  • how to give full and fluent answers to the examiners questions
  • improving your pronunciation

Reading:

  • techniques like skimming and scanning to improve your test reading speed
  • reading about unfamiliar topics in English

Listening:

  • listening test techniques
  • widening your listening range
  • staying focused when you listen

Writing:

  • understanding and describing charts and data
  • writing about trends
  • planning an essay
