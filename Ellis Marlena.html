My passion for teaching English evolved from homeschooling my son when he was four years old. I loved seeing and hearing the results of my child's improved communication skills by my teaching efforts. Although this wasn't my first teaching role. I love watching Netflix, eating Asian food, dancing to house music, and scuba diving. I have had a close up encounter with a Whale Shark and hope one day to go on a Great White Shark cage dive in Guadalupe, Mexico. I'd also love to go to Costa Rica & stay in a jungle treehouse.
